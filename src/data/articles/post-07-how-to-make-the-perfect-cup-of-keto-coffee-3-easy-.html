Mastering the Art of Keto Coffee

Knowing how to make the perfect cup of keto coffee is the difference between loving your morning routine and abandoning it after a week. Here are three proven recipes refined over months of daily testing.

Recipe 1: Classic Bulletproof Keto Coffee

Ingredients: 1 cup brewed keto mushroom coffee, 1 tbsp grass-fed butter (Kerrygold), 1 tbsp MCT oil, pinch of sea salt.

Instructions: Brew coffee. Pour into blender. Add butter, MCT oil, and salt. Blend on high 20-30 seconds until frothy. Pour into pre-warmed mug.

Pro Tip: Coffee must be hot enough to melt the butter before blending. Always blend, never stir.

Recipe 2: Keto Mocha Coffee

Ingredients: 1 cup brewed keto mushroom coffee, 1 tbsp grass-fed butter, 1 tbsp MCT oil, 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder, 1-2 drops liquid stevia.

Instructions: Brew coffee. Add cocoa and splash of hot coffee to blender, blend briefly to dissolve. Add remaining coffee, butter, MCT oil, and stevia. Blend 25-30 seconds.

Pro Tip: Use natural cocoa powder (not Dutch-processed) for more intense chocolate flavor.

Recipe 3: Vanilla Cinnamon Keto Coffee

Ingredients: 1 cup brewed keto mushroom coffee, 1 tbsp grass-fed butter, 1 tbsp MCT oil, 0.5 tsp Ceylon cinnamon, 0.5 tsp pure vanilla extract.

Instructions: Brew coffee with cinnamon in the grounds. Transfer to blender, add butter, MCT oil, vanilla. Blend 25-30 seconds. Garnish with cinnamon.

Pro Tip: Use Ceylon cinnamon (true cinnamon) for better flavor and lower coumarin.

Essential Equipment

A good blender is the most important investment. NutriBullet Pro is excellent and affordable. A milk frother will NOT work.
