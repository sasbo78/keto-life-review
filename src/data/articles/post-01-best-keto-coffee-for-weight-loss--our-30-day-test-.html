What Exactly Is Keto Coffee?

Keto coffee, sometimes called bulletproof coffee, is a high-fat, low-carbohydrate coffee beverage designed to keep your body in a state of ketosis. Unlike regular coffee which is nearly zero-calorie on its own, keto coffee is blended with healthy fats — typically MCT oil, grass-fed butter, or ghee — to create a creamy, satisfying drink that replaces breakfast for many keto dieters. The idea is that by consuming fat instead of carbohydrates in the morning, you keep your insulin levels low, extend your overnight fasting period, and prime your body to burn stored fat for fuel throughout the day.

The specific keto coffee we tested goes a step further by incorporating functional mushroom extracts. Lion's Mane mushroom is known for its cognitive-enhancing properties, supporting memory, focus, and mental clarity. Chaga mushroom is packed with antioxidants and supports immune function. Together, these ingredients create what the manufacturer calls a "functional beverage" — coffee that not only supports weight loss through ketosis but also enhances brain function and overall health.
