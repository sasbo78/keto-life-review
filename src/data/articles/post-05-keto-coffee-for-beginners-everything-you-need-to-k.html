How to Make Keto Coffee: Step-by-Step

Critical rule: you must BLEND it, not stir it.

What you need: 1 cup brewed keto mushroom coffee, 1 tbsp grass-fed butter, 1 tbsp MCT oil, a blender.

Steps: Brew coffee. Pour into blender. Add butter and MCT oil. Blend on high for 20-30 seconds until frothy and creamy. Pour and enjoy.

5 Common Beginner Mistakes

1. Stirring instead of blending. Always use a blender.
2. Using regular butter. Use grass-fed butter (Kerrygold).
3. Adding sugar. Avoid all sweeteners in the first 30 days.
4. Not drinking enough water. Aim for 8-10 glasses per day.
5. Giving up after 3 days. Commit to at least 14 days.
